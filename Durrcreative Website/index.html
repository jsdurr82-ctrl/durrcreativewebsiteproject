ABOUT ME

My journey into videography started early, making home videos and running a YouTube channel called Joemazing Magic. What began as a creative outlet quickly turned into a passion, and by high school I was fully immersed in filming and storytelling.

Now based in Chicago, I work across a range of projects including artists, events, real estate, brands, and weddings. From filming to editing, I focus on creating content that feels polished, intentional, and true to each client's vision. Whether it's capturing a live moment or crafting a story in post, I aim to deliver visuals that stand out.

I'm currently available for freelance projects. Reach out to @durrcreative and let's create something great.
